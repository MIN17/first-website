Grade Calculator

You can type in your grades and weights for each of the assignments and the calculator will tell you what your final grade and percentage will be. It will also tell you what you need to get on the final exam to get a certain grade.

As an undergrad, especially towards the end of semester, you may start worrying about what you need on your final to get a certain grade. This tool will help you figure that out. If you know what the specific cutoffs for each of the grade letters are, you can type them. If not, it will use the following default cutoffs used at UMD:

  • A+: 97%+
  • A: 93-96%
  • A-: 90-92%

  • B+: 87-89%
  • B: 83-86%
  • B-: 80-82%

  • C+: 77-79%
  • C: 73-76%
  • C-: 70-72%

  • D+: 67-69%
  • D: 63-66%
  • D-: 60-62%
  • F: 59%-
